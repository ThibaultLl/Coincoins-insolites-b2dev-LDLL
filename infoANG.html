Application's Presentation - user guide

Coincoins Insolites ;

What the heck is that ?

Coincoins insolite is an application created for hikers !

Sunday hikers, amateurs, or professionals, everyone is invited to use our application ! download-it and go on an excursion alone or with your friends, Coincoins insolites is your application, you will make it live with your discoveries and your tastes.

How does it work ?

Open the application, activate localization. These are the only things you will need to do to get the most out of your hikes. The application will offer you different unusual corners, you can select the radius around you to discover a maximum of cool places. You can share your own corners so that our hiker friends can spot them in turn!

If you do not have a sense of direction, our application will offer you to open a GPS with a precise route to the place you have selected! Play the game!

Do not worry !

You will be located only when the application is active, you can even deactivate the location once you have selected your corner! Our application was not created for profit, it is you who will enrich and feed Coincoins insolites, therefore it is you who will benefit

Our primary goal is to link the hiker community and make everyone want to share this hobby, this passion! We also want to support the user experience, therefore;

  • No adds !
  • An application to download for free !
  • No paid functionality, everything will be free!
  • And of course, a community in constant communication, that's why everyone can post their own quirky corners, and, why not, be highlighted!

    • Our Society :

    We are a group of four students from Campus Academy Nantes, and we are carrying out a project called "Coincoins insolite" consisting of creating an application of sharing of unusual places and a website presenting it.
